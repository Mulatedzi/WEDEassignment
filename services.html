Our Services

Comprehensive water solutions designed to meet the unique needs of communities across South Africa.

What We Offer

From cutting-edge purification technology to grassroots education, our services cover every aspect of water access and sustainability.

Water Purification Systems

We design, install, and maintain advanced water purification stations using multi-stage filtration, UV sterilisation, and reverse osmosis technology. Our systems are solar-powered and built to withstand local environmental conditions.

Includes: Installation, maintenance, water quality testing, and replacement parts.

Conservation Education

Our educational programmes teach schools and communities about water conservation, hygiene, and sustainable water management. We develop customised curricula for different age groups and community contexts.

Includes: Workshops, training materials, school programmes, and community seminars.

Community Outreach

We work directly with communities to assess needs, deploy resources, and build local capacity. Our outreach teams partner with traditional leaders, schools, and local organisations to ensure lasting impact.

Includes: Needs assessment, community engagement, volunteer training, and ongoing support.

Additional Programmes

Beyond our core services, we offer specialised programmes to address specific community needs.

Emergency Water Response

Rapid deployment of mobile purification units during droughts, floods, or infrastructure failures. Our emergency teams can be on-site within 24 hours to provide immediate clean water access to affected communities.

Water Quality Testing

Comprehensive water testing services for communities, schools, and municipalities. We test for bacteria, heavy metals, chemical contaminants, and other pollutants, providing detailed reports and remediation recommendations.

Corporate Partnerships

We partner with businesses to fulfil their CSI mandates through meaningful water projects. Our team handles project design, implementation, reporting, and impact measurement for corporate sponsors.

Government Collaboration

We work alongside municipal and provincial governments to supplement public water infrastructure, particularly in areas where government resources are stretched. Our partnerships are transparent and aligned with national water policy.

Youth Water Steward Training

A vocational programme that trains young people from underserved communities to become certified water technicians and community educators, creating employment while building local expertise.

Research & Development

We continuously research and test new water technologies, from low-cost ceramic filters to atmospheric water generators, to find the most effective solutions for South African conditions.
