## Project Overview

**Water For All** is a non-profit organisation dedicated to providing sustainable access to clean, safe drinking water for underserved communities across South Africa. This project delivers a complete 5-page website for the organisation, built with semantic HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript.

The website serves as a digital presence to raise awareness about water scarcity, showcase the organisation's services, facilitate community engagement through enquiry forms, and provide contact information for multiple office locations.

---

## Website Goals and Objectives

- **Increase Awareness:** Raise public awareness about water scarcity in South Africa
- **Generate Leads:** Attract enquiries from communities, volunteers, and sponsors
- **Provide Information:** Serve as a comprehensive hub about services and impact
- **Build Credibility:** Establish trust through transparent reporting
- **Facilitate Engagement:** Enable volunteering, sponsorship, and service requests

## Design Specifications

### Colour Palette

| Colour | Hex | Usage |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Primary Blue | #0077B6 | Headings, links, buttons |
| Deep Blue | #023E8A | Dark sections, footer, H2 |
| Teal Accent | #00B4D8 | CTA buttons, highlights |
| Light Blue | #90E0EF | Hover effects, accents |
| Pale Blue | #CAF0F8 | Card backgrounds |
| Dark Text | #1A1A2E | Body text |
| Grey Text | #6C757D | Captions, secondary |

### Typography

- **Font Family:** 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif
- **Headings:** Bold, H1=2.5rem, H2=2rem, H3=1.5rem
- **Body:** Regular, 16px, line-height 1.6
- **Captions:** 0.9rem, grey colour

## Pages

### 1. Homepage (index.html)
- Hero section with water background and CTA buttons
- Impact statistics with animated counters
- Three feature cards (Purification, Education, Outreach)
- Mission statement
- Call-to-action section

### 2. About Us (about.html)

- Mission and vision statements

### 3. Services (services.html)

### 4. Enquiry (enquiry.html)

- FAQ section

### 5. Contact (contact.html)
- Contact information and office hours
- Contact form with subject categories
- Three office locations (Cape Town, Durban, Polokwane)
- Map placeholder and social media links

## Features

- **Responsive Design:** Fully responsive from 320px to 1200px+ breakpoints
- **Mobile Navigation:** Hamburger menu on screens below 768px
- **Form Validation:** Real-time validation with clear error messages
- **Accessibility:** Skip links, semantic HTML, ARIA labels, WCAG AA contrast
- **Interactive Elements:** Animated counters, hover effects, scroll-to-top button
- **Cross-browser Compatible:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
