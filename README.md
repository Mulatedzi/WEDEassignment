 Project Overview
Water For All is a website developed for a fictional company based in Polokwane, South Africa. The company focuses on providing clean and affordable water to communities experiencing water shortages.

The website provides information about the business, its services, and how customers can get in contact.


  Website Goals and Objectives
- Provide information about water supply services
- Raise awareness about water shortages in Polokwane
- Offer contact details for customers
- Present the company professionally online
- Support future expansion into other water-scarce areas
