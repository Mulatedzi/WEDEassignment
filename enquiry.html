Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about getting involved with Water For All.

How long does it take to install a water purification system?

Depending on the community size and system type, installation typically takes 2-4 weeks from approval, including site assessment, community consultation, and equipment setup.

Do you charge communities for your services?

Our core services are provided free of charge to underserved communities. We are funded through donations, corporate sponsorships, and government grants.

What qualifications do I need to volunteer?

No specific qualifications are required. We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds and provide comprehensive training for all roles.

How can my company become a sponsor?

Contact us through this form selecting "Sponsorship" and our partnerships team will reach out to discuss tailored sponsorship opportunities.

Prefer to Call?

You can also reach us directly during office hours, Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm.
