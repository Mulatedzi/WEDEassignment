Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?
Depending on community size, installation typically takes 2-4 weeks from approval.

Do you charge communities?
Our core services are provided free of charge. We are funded through donations, sponsorships, and grants.

What qualifications do volunteers need?
No specific qualifications are required. We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds and provide training.

How can my company become a sponsor?
Select "Sponsorship" in the form and our partnerships team will reach out to discuss opportunities.
